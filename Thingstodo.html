Visit Fifth Ave


Playa del Carmen’s famous 5th Avenue is a popular tourist attraction with high-end retailers, bars, restaurants, local shops, cafes, street entertainment and more. No one who has ever stepped on it can deny how impressive it is to walk the entire avenue. You’ll find a wide variety of stores on 5th Avenue, ranging from American brands to flea market-type stalls with handmade souvenirs. There are many great dining choices on 5th Avenue. Keep in mind that many of the restaurants are geared to tourists, so you’ll see higher prices compared to eateries off the main drag. For coffe-lovers, Ah Cacao, is one of the most famous and beloved cafes in Playa del Carmen, thanks to its 100% Mexican and organic chocolate and coffee. The best part this street is just a few steps away from the beach. In fact it is common to see locals and tourists in swimwear walking around the area. Those who stay at the 5th Avenue have the opportunity to explore the local culture, visit the best shops, as well as visit the beach after strolling down the avenue.


Visit the beach


You can bring your own beach towels/chairs/etc. Or you can visit, Mamitas Beach Club. It sits in the middle of Playa Del Carmen in the trendy neighborhood just about three blocks from 5th Avenue. This beach club was what helped Playa del Carmen party scene get started.
