Why you should go:


If you’re looking for a trendy, beautiful trip, visit Playa del Carmen. Originally a small fishing town, Playa del Carmen, or Playa, as it’s more affectionately known, has undergone rapid development with its luxury buildings, restaurants and boutiques to become the must-see destination that it is. A Playa del Carmen travel experience includes a lot of modern adventures, like top-par people watching, shopping along Fifth Avenue, and spending a day on an excursion



When to go:


Due to its location, Playa del Carmen is known for its hot summers and warm, mild winters, making it one of the best vacation destinations regardless of the time of year.



Where to stay:


They're many various options to stay in playa. If you want a more immersed cultral experience, there are various Airbnbs options to choose from. If you’re looking for a quiet, private place, you’ll be sure to find an adults-only hotel tucked along a private stretch of beach. Or, if you’re booking a family vacation, you’ll be able to find a large resort with plenty of pools or nearby activities to make sure the whole family stays entertained. There’s so much to see and do in Playa del Carmen but the first step is to make sure you have a place to stay, that’s right for you.
